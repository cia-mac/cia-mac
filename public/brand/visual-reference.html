§ 02 · Wordmark

One mark. Uppercase. Barless A.

  • One line. Never stack or break. Uniform Light (200) weight — no letter emphasis.
  • The barless A lives only in the mark. Never in running text, headings, or labels.
  • No monogram. No standalone symbol, no “CIA” mark. The wordmark is the mark at every size.
  • Case in prose stays lowercase: ciamac.com, “the ciamac brand”. Formal long form: Ciamac Parhizi.
  • currentColor only. NASA worm (1975) is the precedent for the missing stroke.
