A reading companion

Vision Science, chapter by chapter

Built to sit beside Stephen E. Palmer, Vision Science: Photons to Phenomenology (MIT Press, 1999) — read the chapter, then turn the idea with your hands.

This follows the book's own order, not impact order. Each chapter lists its load-bearing ideas; where one has an interactive, it's linked. The spine is the book's argument, restated once per chapter: vision is not recording — it is construction.
