The mixture

There is no yellow light on this screen. Set red and green high, blue to zero: the centre reads as yellow because that mixture excites your L and M cones in the same ratio a single 580 nm wavelength would. The percept is identical; the physics is not. Colour is not a property of light — it is a construction of the visual system.
