let's make a remix

You'll turn Googoosh's voice into your own track in 5 small steps. I'll walk you through each one at the bottom of the screen — no music experience needed.

  1. Load her voice — drop in your pol-vocal.wav
  2. Find a moment you love by listening
  3. Put it on a pad and play it with a key
  4. Add a beat underneath
  5. Record your take to a file
🎧 tip: use headphones, and work in Chrome
